# üçï Pizza Sales Data Analysis

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing pizza sales data to extract **meaningful business insights** related to revenue, customer demand, and ordering patterns.  
The analysis supports **data-driven decision-making** for improving sales performance and operational efficiency.

---

## Business Objectives
- Analyze **overall sales performance** and revenue trends  
- Identify **peak ordering hours** and high-demand days  
- Understand **customer preferences** based on pizza category and size  
- Identify **top and bottom-performing products**

---

## Dataset
This project uses a **real-world pizza sales dataset** sourced from Kaggle for **educational and analytical purposes**.  
The dataset includes order details, order times, pizza categories, sizes, quantities, and pricing information.

---

## Tools & Technologies
- **SQL** ‚Äì Data querying, aggregation, and KPI calculation  
- **Microsoft Excel** ‚Äì Dashboard creation using Pivot Tables and charts  

---

## Key KPIs Calculated
- **Total Revenue**  
- **Average Order Value**  
- **Total Orders**  
- **Total Pizzas Sold**  
- **Average Pizzas per Order**

---

## Analysis Performed
- Performed **revenue and order volume analysis** using SQL  
- Conducted **daily trend analysis** to identify high-order days  
- Conducted **hourly trend analysis** to determine peak business hours  
- Analyzed **sales contribution by pizza category and size**  
- Identified **top 5 and bottom 5 best-selling pizzas**

---

## üí° Key Insights
- **Peak orders** occur during evening hours, especially between **6 PM and 8 PM**  
- **Large-sized pizzas** contribute the highest share of total revenue  
- A **small number of top-selling pizzas** generate a significant portion of overall sales  
- Certain **pizza categories consistently outperform** others in terms of demand  

---

## üìå Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing campaigns during **peak evening hours**  
- Promote **high-performing pizza categories and sizes**  
- Introduce **targeted offers** for low-performing pizzas to improve sales
